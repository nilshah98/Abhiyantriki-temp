About

Startup Expo is a convention for young entrepreneurs, being organized Abhiyantriki 2017 to promote entrepreneurship. It features a plethora of events including guest lectures, elevator pitch, networking meets, panel discussions, and more.

Format & Details

  • It is a two day event to be held on 6th and 7th of October 2017 at K.J. Somaiya College of Engineering. This event is being organised by Team Abhiyantriki, Riidl and Bloombox.
  • Any number of Startups can participate in the Startup Expo but only top 20 startups will be selected by the internal panel.
  • On Day 1(6th Oct) these 20 startups will deliver their pitch in front of a small panel. Out of these 20 startups, 5 will be selected for the next round which will take place on Day 2(7th Oct).
  • On Day 2, these 5 startups will pitch in front of an elite panel(consisting of Riidl, Investors, Mentors, Co-working spaces). Points will be awarded based on certain criterias, and accordingly the winners of Startup Expo will be decided.
  • Discussion time will be given on Day 2 after the 5 pitches end. In this time RiiDL or mentors would talk to startups they select and could lead to the incubators finally incubating the startup.
